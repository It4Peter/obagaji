OPIATOHA G'OBAGAJI

Opiatoha g'Obagaji is a non profit organization dedicated to the community both in Otukpo, Obagaji and Agatu. It is an association of people who were living in Obagaji before but are now residing in Otukpo. Even though we are currently living in Otukpo, we decided to organized ourselves to carry out projects that will promote peace and development at our alma-matter. The association was founded in April 2019. The first Chairman of the association was Mr. Ojema Olobo Joseph and the current Chairman is Mr. Oyishoma Samson.

The association have their meetings every fourteen (14) days (Sundays). Currently, Okabido Hotel, Otukpo is the venue where we meet for each meeting days. It is our pleasure for all Obagaji residents who are now in Otukpo to be part of this organization. Together we will be able to help ourselves and those we left behind in Agatu through our numerous programmes, they will know we have not forgotten and will never forget where we came from.

MEMBERSHIP

  • Membership of this association is open not only to those who are of Obagaji origins, but to those who had domiciled in Obagaji for five years or more but are now in Otukpo.
  • Wife or husband of anyone that is qualified to be a member is also eligible to be a member regardless of whether he or she has lived in Obagaji before, as long as his/her current location is in Otukpo.
